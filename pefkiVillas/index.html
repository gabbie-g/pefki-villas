GREAT SUMMER VACATION

GREAT SUMMER VACATION

Pefki is considered one of the premier vacation destinations in North Evia, since it has a highly developed tourist infrastructure and offers excellent hotel accommodation, rooms, apartments and studios throughout the course of the year. If you want a relaxing break away from the hectic pace of everyday life, Pefki is the perfect place.
